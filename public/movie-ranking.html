The Best Movies According to Israel

Top 3 Movies of all-time

Good Will Hunting

A beautiful representation of an emotionally closed-off and immature genius who struggles to be vulnerable with those he loves. Demonstrates the inner thoughts of a self destructive individual tasked attempting to partake in meaningful relationships.

Spider-Man 2

Demonstrates what being a hero means and shows the inner struggles of a hero who never asked to be given such a responsibility

Howl's Moving Castle

Someone very special to me showed me this film and it will always hold a special place in my heart.
